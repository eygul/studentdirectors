    Student Directors

    We the student directors pledge our commitment to the students of Berea College labor program, providing support to all departments with clear communication, transparency, and integrity, paying special attention to service.

    What we do Meet the Student Directors

    Some of the Things We Do

    As Student Directors, we do many things. Here are some of the examples:

    01

    Birthday Cards

    In order to better celebrate the labor students of Berea College, we make hand-written and specialized birthday cards for each student.

    02

    Provide Resources

    No matter what training or other mediums of educational resources labor students may need, Student Directors are prepared to deliver them.

    03

    Labor Meetings

    As Student Directors, we join labor meetings to meet with and get to know about labor students on a deeper level. This allows us to learn more about students' needs.
